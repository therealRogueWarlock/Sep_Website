Asimo

We are very exited to announce our current colaboration with Honda. We are working with Honda to produce a working AI for their Autonomous Robot; Asimo.
What we have been doing on Asimo is training the AI to perform multiple different tasks, from playing football to telling questionable jokes.
We hope to continue working with the Asimo team at Honda to perfect the Robot and one day get a mass production going. This is by far our largest project to date and we are proud of our efforts contributing to such a massive undertaking.
